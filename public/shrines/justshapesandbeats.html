What's jsab and hw d yu play it?


Taken from Wikipedia©
Just Shapes & Beats is an action rhythm video game developed and published by Canadian indie team Berzerk Studio. It was released on May 31, 2018, for Windows and Nintendo Switch, February 24, 2019, for macOS and Linux, May 10, 2019, for PlayStation 4 in the US, and May 30, 2019 for PlayStation 4 in Europe, Russia, and Australia. It was also released on June 30, 2020, for Stadia, and on May 31, 2022, for Xbox One and Xbox Series X/S. In this game, players control a colored shape and dodge pink shapes that move to the beat of background music.

Why did you keep cming back?


I kept coming back because of the beats, the multiplayer, the party modes, the dark story, the lost chapter and overall it's endless fun. I still have some levels I need to unlock with "beat points" but I really don't care as this is great.
